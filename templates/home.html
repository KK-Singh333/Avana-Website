AVANA

STRIVING FOR CHANGE

IIT Indore

Avana, the social welfare club of IIT Indore, is dedicated to making a difference through impactful initiatives. From organizing blood donation drives and distributing clothes and books to visiting old age homes, orphanages, and teaching local children, Avana embodies the spirit of service and compassion. Its projects aim to bring smiles, foster learning, and support the underprivileged, creating a ripple effect of positive change in the community.

Cloth Donation Drive

Clothes donated by the students and faculty of IIT Indore were distributed to workers and local villagers, fostering a sense of community and care.

Blood Donation Camp

Avana’s blood donation camp saw enthusiastic participation from IIT Indore students, showcasing their commitment to saving lives.

Old Age Home Visit

Avana’s old age home visit brought joy and companionship to the elderly, fostering meaningful connections and spreading warmth.

Plasma Donation

Avana’s plasma donation initiative encouraged IIT Indore students to donate plasma, offering a lifeline to patients.

Life Changing Sessions

Avana held psychologist-led sessions on self-awareness, decision-making, overcoming self-doubt, and resilience, fostering personal growth.
