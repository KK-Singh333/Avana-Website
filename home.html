AVANA

STRIVING FOR CHANGE

IIT Indore

Avana, the social welfare club of IIT Indore, is dedicated to making a difference through impactful initiatives. From organizing blood donation drives and distributing clothes and books to visiting old age homes, orphanages, and teaching local children, Avana embodies the spirit of service and compassion. Its projects aim to bring smiles, foster learning, and support the underprivileged, creating a ripple effect of positive change in the community.
